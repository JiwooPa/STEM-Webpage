How Do You Do the Experiment?

  1. Dip the clean wire loop into the powdered metal salt.
    Alternatively, dissolve a little salt in water and dip the wire into the solution.
  2. Hold the loop in the edge of the blue flame (hottest part of the flame).
  3. Observe and record the flame color.
  4. Clean the wire between tests using hydrochloric acid (HCl), then heat again to remove residue.
  5. Repeat the process for different metal salts and record the results.
